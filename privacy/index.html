Privacy Policy

Our covenant on the protection of your royal data. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully as it governs how we collect, use, and protect your information.

The Royal Data We Collect How We Use Your Royal Data Sharing Within the Kingdom Protecting the Royal Vault Your Royal Rights Cookies and Tracking Edicts International Data Transfers Children of the Kingdom Changes to This Scroll Contact Us
1. The Royal Data We Collect

In the Kingdom of Sorteroyal, we hold your privacy in the highest regard. This Royal Privacy Scroll explains how we collect, use, protect, and store your information as you enjoy our gaming realm.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Your royal name and title (username)
  • Email address for royal communications
  • Date of birth to verify your right to enter the kingdom
  • Preferences and settings for your gaming experience
  • Voluntary feedback, surveys, or suggestions you provide to improve our realm

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (type, operating system, browser version)
  • Gameplay statistics, scores, achievements, and session history
  • IP address, approximate location, and login times (for security)
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see our Cookie Edict)
2. How We Use Your Royal Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize your gaming experience
  • Maintain the security of our kingdom and protect against fraud
  • Respond to your royal inquiries and customer support requests
  • Improve our games, introduce new features, and enhance the realm’s design
  • Send important kingdom announcements, newsletters, or promotional events (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations and ensure responsible gaming practices
3. Sharing Within the Kingdom

We may share your information with:

  • Trusted royal advisors (service providers) who help operate our kingdom, such as hosting and analytics partners
  • Other nobles during gameplay, where usernames, avatars, and achievements may be displayed
  • Legal authorities when required by royal decree (law or regulation)
  • New owners or successors if the kingdom is transferred or merged

We never sell your personal information to outsiders, merchants, or advertisers. Your royal identity remains sacred.

4. Protecting the Royal Vault

We implement strong measures to safeguard your information, such as:

  • Encryption of sensitive details during transmission and storage
  • Regular security audits and monitoring by digital sentinels
  • Strict access/controls so only trusted guardians may handle your data
  • Secure servers housed within fortified data castles

While no fortress is impenetrable, we strive to ensure your information is guarded with the highest standards of digital defense.

5. Your Royal Rights

As a noble of our kingdom, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or outdated details
  • Delete your account and associated records from our vaults
  • Object to certain processing activities or withdraw consent
  • Request a copy of your information in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact our Royal Data Protector at privacy@sorteroyal.com.

6. Cookies and Tracking Edicts

Our cookies serve as loyal messengers, remembering your preferences and helping us improve your royal journey. Some cookies are essential for gameplay, while others enhance performance and allow us to measure audience engagement. You may choose to decline non-essential cookies, but certain features of the kingdom may not function as intended.

7. International Data Transfers

As our kingdom spans multiple realms, your data may be transferred and processed beyond your homeland. We ensure such transfers comply with international protection laws and that safeguards are in place to preserve your noble rights.

8. Children of the Kingdom

Our services are intended for nobles aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from younger subjects. If we discover that a minor has entered the court, their data will be removed without delay.

9. Changes to This Scroll

We may update this Royal Privacy Scroll periodically to reflect new laws, technologies, or kingdom practices. Significant changes will be announced through royal proclamation on our website. Continued use of our services signifies your acceptance of these updated terms.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team at support@sorteroyal.com or call +92 300 1234567.
