1. Cookies Overview

At Sorteroyal, we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our social gaming platform. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your options for managing them. By using our website and services, you consent to the use of cookies as described below.

2. Types of Cookies Used

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function, enabling core features like user authentication and game functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymized data on how users interact with our platform to improve performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences (e.g., language or game settings) to provide a personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user behavior, such as which games are most popular, to enhance our services.
3. Purpose of Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure smooth operation of our social gaming platform and secure online play.
  • Personalize your gaming experience by remembering your preferences and settings.
  • Analyze platform usage to improve game performance and introduce new features.
  • Support responsible gaming by monitoring user interactions and ensuring a safe environment.

We do not use cookies to collect personal information for sale or advertising purposes.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to disable non-essential cookies or delete existing cookies. However, disabling cookies may limit some features of our platform, such as personalized game settings or performance optimizations. For more information, visit your browser’s help section.

You can also manage cookie preferences via our cookie consent popup when you first visit our site, choosing to accept or decline non-essential cookies.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services (e.g., analytics or hosting providers) that set their own cookies. These providers follow strict privacy guidelines and only use data necessary for their services. We ensure these third parties comply with applicable data protection laws.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or our services. Significant changes will be posted on our website. Continued use of our services after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
